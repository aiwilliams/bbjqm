This sample shows that "data-prefetch" will not allow us to obtain all Pages while online such that the application could work in an offline mode.

Since we are using AJAX Single Page loading, once we leave a Page it is deleted from the DOM and forgotten altogether. This causes the Page to be reloaded or prefetched again when it is linked to with data-prefetch.

In fact, when you go from index to level1, and then press you're back button, you will see that level1 is prefetched again!
